DATABAND + INSTANA

Integration Incubtator

Product story

Objective

My team was tasked with running exploratory research to uncover connections between two IBM products, Databand and Instana, to identify and validate cross product solutions and integrations for a larger observability story.

Automation executives need a way to quickly understand if there are viable use cases for how data engineers and devops engineers overlap and work together and understand how these two products could best work together to serve the observability market before staffing a full product and engineering team.

Personas

DevOps SRE

Primary job: maintain infrastructure health and reduce MTTR

  • Builds software to help operations and support teams
  • Monitors, manages, and runs distributed systems
  • Reduces toil by automating tasks that require a human operator to manually work on a system
  • Sets SLO/SLIs and introduces error budgets to measure risk and balance availability and feature development

Data engineer

Primary job: migrate and transform data

  • Manages day-to-day pipelines, including reviewing error logs and pipeline execution
  • Models solutions for data analysts and scientists
  • Designs, implements, and maintains data and integration tests for data quality
  • Evaluations and recommends technology

Process

Timeline summary

SCOPE WORK AND DEVELOP PLAN

  • Develop research plan breaking into 2 studies
  • Create screener for Data Engineers and DevOps
  • Interview 16 participants to understand their roles
  • Synthesise 2 studies and then compare to find potential overlap between Data engineers and DevOps
  • Define use cases and pain points

CREATE MULTIPLE POTENTIAL INTEGRATION PROPOSALS

  • Develop big ideas that correspond with pain points for each need statement
  • Build midfi screens to show potential user flows showing optimal ways Databand and Instana can work together with a range of integration
  • Meet with product management and lead developers for both products to discuss feasibility and refine concepts

TEST CONCEPTS WITH USERS AND MARKET

  • Set up interviews with potential users, analysts, and sales
  • Create mural flow to walk through 7 potential concepts
  • Have participants rank concepts
  • Synthesise results for final proposal

FINAL PLAYBACK WITH PROPOSAL

  • Playback high level breakdown of work and then propose next steps for moving forward
  • Meet with product management and lead developers for both product teams to discuss concepts and open door for deeper discussion.

Details of generative research

For this effort we conducted interviews with two different user groups at the same time, using a set of similar questions. The goal was to compare perspectives and use cases that bridged across both the data engineer and devOps perspectives. We interviewed 8 Data engineers and 9 DevOps engineers.

UNDERSTAND DATA ENGINEER AND DEVOPS RESPONSIBILITIES

Identify where both roles collaborate and if there are opportunities for creating better visibility between roles to help outline 2-3 common use cases with pain points and opportunities to improve how they work today

HAVE PARTICIPANTS DEFINE WHAT OBSERVABILITY MEANS TO THEIR ROLE

Discovery how data engineers and devops define observability as it relates to their role to see if there is overlap in terminology between roles

MAP RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN TEAM STRUCTURE AND COMMON TOOLS

Find any overlaps in tools used by data engineers and devops and understand team structure to see collaboration points
