JUMPSTART INNOVATION TEAM

Create and lead an incubation team

Management story

Role

UX Design lead

Objective

Design and product management executives need a way to explore forward thinking opportunities so they don't waste unnecessary time and resources, but most design teams do not have the skills, structure, or bandwidth to explore and incubate future opportunities.

The Jumpstart Innovation team creates "UX concept cars" that distill multiple innovative ideas into broad designs to guide future versions of IBM Automation products into more competitive and strategic directions by utilizing a multi-disciplinary design team with required bandwidth, experience, and structure that traditional product teams do not have.

Personas

Vice President of Product Management

Primary need: Fast, reliable way to validate and incubate new ideas products and capabilities

  • Can't commit production resources to exploration efforts
  • Doesn't want to risk wasting time and money on building the wrong things
  • Needs validation of market, buyers, customers, and users
  • Needs multiple options for implementation direction

Vice President of Design

Primary need: Insure user experience is considered early in the process of creating new products and key capabilities

  • Needs to avoid products and capabilities being planned without concern for the end user experience
  • Needs to validate concepts with proper user testing
  • Needs product to deliver reliability, usability, and delight on top of functionality

Process

(3 months)

Key activities

FORMED A TEAM

  • Recruited and trained a cross-functional team
  • Formalized a team mission, operational processes, and OKRs
  • Aligned with executive sponsors from VP to CEO for prioritization
  • Scoped requirements for each project

LED PROJECTS AT 3 MONTH CADENCE

  • Incubated first version of watsonx Orchestrate and collateral for THINK mainstage demo
  • Investigated integration opportunities for watsonx Orchestrate with Watson AIOps, Instana, and Turbonomic
  • Investigated integration opportunities for fullstack observability platform with Databand and Instana
  • Future of work strategic foresight to cast 5+ year vision for possible strategic direction
  • Incubated productization of open source BAMOE project

CONTINUOUSLY IMPROVED PROCESS

  • Collaborated with vice presidents to make sure their primary goals are met
  • Led retrospectives on team
  • Reviewed and iterated on key processes between each project
  • Provided continuous upskilling to team such as strategic foresight, executive presentations, user research methodologies
